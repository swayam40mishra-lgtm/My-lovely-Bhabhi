A Personal Note

A Letter from Deverji

Not every bond needs years to become meaningful. Sometimes, a single month is enough for someone to quietly become unforgettable.

For You

I don’t think I can ever fully explain how much your presence has started to mean to me. Just a little while ago — on 5 March — I saw you for the first time, and somehow, in such a short time, you became an important part of my world.

You gave me something rare: a space where I could speak honestly, think more clearly, and feel understood without having to explain too much.

Sometimes, you guide me in ways that stay with me long after the conversation ends. And sometimes, simply talking to you feels enough to make everything feel lighter.

Thank you — for your patience, your kindness, your words, and even for tolerating all my messages… even the slightly annoying ones 😁

This is only the beginning. There is something more waiting beyond this page.
