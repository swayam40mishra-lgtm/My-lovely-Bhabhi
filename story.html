A Personal Message

This is not just a page…
it is something I made,
only for you.

A few words. A few memories. A few feelings I never wanted to leave unsaid.

For Renu Mishra

You were never just my bhabhi…

Some people enter your life with a role. But a few stay with you as a feeling.

You became someone I could always talk to.

In moments when I had too much on my mind, you somehow made things feel lighter — just by listening.

Even when life kept you busy… you still replied.

That may seem small to the world, but to me, it always meant more than you know.

You never made me feel ignored.

You gave attention to my words, respect to my feelings, and warmth to my silence.

You guided me… the way a mother would.

Not by force. Not by pressure. But with patience, care, and quiet understanding.

And somewhere along the way… you changed me.

Maybe not in one dramatic moment, but in a hundred small ones that slowly became unforgettable.

You became one of the safest people in my life.

And in a world that changes so quickly, that kind of comfort is rare… and priceless.

A Note From My Heart

You may have entered my life as my bhabhi… but in my heart, you became so much more: someone I respect deeply, someone I trust completely, and someone I will always be grateful for.
